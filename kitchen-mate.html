What it does in plain language

  • Shows what the table has before food spoils.
  • Suggests meals from what is already available.
  • Respects vibe cooking instead of forcing rigid recipes.
  • Flags use-by risk and allergen questions before sharing.
  • Turns leftovers into future value, not guilt.
