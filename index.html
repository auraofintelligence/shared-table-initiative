Working map

Four public bridges around one table.

The Shared Table is the food and gathering story. The Noticeboard turns calls for help into public-safe notices. The Community Ledger records opt-in contribution and support trails. The Grants Lab turns the evidence into fundable applications and reporting packs.

Prototype boundary

Grounded enough to discuss, careful enough not to over-claim.

The source brief is ambitious: regenerative food systems, C-Hours, AI pantry support, digital twins, cultural protocol, local media and grant pathways. This website turns that into a public-friendly structure without pretending the whole system already exists.

Before any real event, the next step is not hype. It is consent, cultural review, food safety review, venue permission, insurance, clear leadership and a small trial scope.
