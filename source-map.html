Hero: shared coastal table

Photorealistic editorial website hero image for a community food-sharing initiative on Minjerribah / North Stradbroke Island, Australia. Golden-hour outdoor shared table under casuarina trees near the coast, simple timber table with fresh local produce, herbs, bread, fish, bowls, reusable crates, and people's hands placing food down together. Warm, grounded, practical, joyful responsible abundance. No corporate look, no logos, no readable text, no QR codes, no signs, no fantasy elements. Wide landscape composition, strong empty space on the left for web headline, crisp natural lighting, realistic Australian coastal vegetation, professional documentary photography, high detail.
