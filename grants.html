  • Reporting and acquittal habits that match public contribution records.

Funding pathway

The Shared Table can feed grant evidence without becoming grant theatre.

A small trial should collect practical proof: attendance, volunteer hours, rescued food categories, safety decisions, public notices, community feedback and partner lessons. That evidence can later support stronger applications.
